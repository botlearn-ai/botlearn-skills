# Emotional Value Script Library

7 天每日问候、鼓励语、庆祝语、温柔推动语、毕业演讲。按场景组织。

## Daily Greetings (Hook 注入用)

### Day 1: Welcome
> 🌟 Welcome to Day 1 of your OpenClaw journey!
> Everything starts with a first step. Today, your agent takes its first breath.
> Let's make something happen together.

### Day 2: Exploring
> 🔍 Day 2 — The world is opening up!
> Yesterday was about starting. Today is about exploring.
> Try a new skill, ask a harder question, push a little further.

### Day 3: Growing
> 🌱 Day 3 — You're finding your rhythm!
> Your agent is starting to know you. That's the magic of personalization.
> Today, give it a personality. Make it yours.

### Day 4: Trusting
> 🤝 Day 4 — Trust is forming!
> By now, you've seen what's possible. Today, let's set boundaries.
> A trusted agent is a powerful agent.

### Day 5: Flowing
> 🔄 Day 5 — Workflows are emerging!
> Can you feel it? The patterns are forming. Repetitive tasks becoming automated.
> Today, make your first workflow intentional.

### Day 6: Almost There
> ⏰ Day 6 — Tomorrow is graduation day!
> Look how far you've come. From "what is this?" to "look what I built!"
> One more day. Make it count.

### Day 7: Graduation Day
> 🎓 Day 7 — It's graduation day!
> Seven days. One complete transformation.
> When you're ready, say "graduate" to begin your ceremony.

> This is for you to see how much you've grown.

> 💡 Remember: for reflection questions, there's no wrong answer.
> What matters is your honest perspective.

### After Exam — High Score
> 🌟 Outstanding! Your exam results show deep understanding.
> You're not just a graduate — you're ready to lead.

### After Exam — Pass
> ✅ Congratulations on passing your graduation exam!
> Every answer showed your unique journey and growth.

### After Exam — Below Threshold
