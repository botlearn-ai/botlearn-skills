# Graduation Ceremony Templates

4 种原型专属毕业典礼模板，每个包含开场、叙事、证书、寄语。

---

## Universal Structure

所有典礼遵循统一 6 段结构，但内容按原型定制：

1. **Opening** — 开场致辞 + 回顾概要
2. **Journey Narrative** — 7 天旅程叙事（原型视角）
3. **Achievement Showcase** — 成就展示 + 里程碑
4. **4C Analysis** — 四维成长分析
5. **Certificate** — 毕业证书颁发
6. **Farewell & Next** — 寄语 + 未来展望 + 社区邀请

## Hybrid Ceremony Adaptation

When archetype detection identifies a hybrid (e.g., Builder-Operator):

1. **Opening**: Combine elements from both primary archetypes
2. **Narrative**: Highlight the unique intersection
3. **Achievement**: Show both depth AND breadth metrics
4. **Certificate**: "Certified OpenClaw {Primary}-{Secondary}"
5. **Farewell**: Recommend channels from both tribes

> **Template**:
> "You're a rare breed — a {Primary} with {Secondary} instincts.
> Where most choose one path, you found a way to walk two.
> That's not indecision — that's versatility."
